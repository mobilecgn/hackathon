What you can expect

Create with other designer and developers new ideas for the smartwatch. Implement your ideas as rapid prototyp within four hours. And sure, you can join other teams with great ideas as well as pitch your own ideas at the beginning of the hackathon.

We know that any team / project will profit from people with different backgrounds! So, anybody who could constribute something is absolutly welcome. Android developers as well as people with Ideas, Concepts, Designs or small backends.

Everything is possible

Adopt your existing Android app to Android Wear or create something new. Open doors, connect other devices or people, display your car position, ...
Be creative!

With the pleasure of our sponsors we provide you
with test devices and other hardware to play, like iBeacons.

Schedule

We open the doors for you in the Solution Space at 2 pm and start the hackathon at 3 pm with some great talks about the opportunities. Get some inspiration before you start your 4 hour venture.

14:00 Doors open, meet people, time to kicker, first beer
15:00 Kickoff Hackthon
15:30 The vision talk: Wearables today & tomorrow, Ralph Christoph
16:00 The tech talk: Android Wear introduction, Pascal Welsch and Frederik Schweiger
16:30 Pitch your ideas, create or join a team
17:00 Start hackin’
21:00 Finish hackin’ incl. some slides. Present your results!

Speakers

The vision talk: Wearables today & tomorrow

Ralph Christoph is a founding member and head of strategy of the c/o pop festival and convention in Cologne. We are happy that he will give us an short non technical vision talk about the future of wearables.

The tech talk: Android Wear introduction

Pascal Welsch and Frederik Schweiger are great Android developers and will give us the technical introduction into the Android Wear ecosystem.
